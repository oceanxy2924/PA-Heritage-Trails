Peranakan Trail

Peranakan culture in Singapore has roots tracing all the way back to Sir Stamford Raffles’ arrival in 1819.

However, despite the initial hype after Channel 8’s wildly popular drama “The Little Nyonya”, Peranakan culture is slowly ‘losing its soul’.
